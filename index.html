Understanding the Concept

Our idea translates the fundamental elements of art—Dots, Lines, and Shapes—into a highly functional, ergonomic study desk. Rather than randomly adding features, every aspect of the desk is derived directly from these basic geometries. Here is how:

The Dot (Junctions & Focus)

Dots act as focal points and connections. In our desk, the 'dots' are the physical joints where legs meet the table, clean cable management pass-throughs, and the circular flush-mounted wireless charging hub for your devices.

The Line (Posture & Flow)

Lines dictate movement and direction. The sweeping carved lines along the edge of the desk provide an organic resting curve for your wrists. The long, continuous lines of the aluminum legs enable smooth height adjustments for optimal posture.

The Shape (Structure & Surface)

Shapes combine dots and lines into stable structures. We utilize heavy triangular bases (the strongest structural shape in engineering) for maximum stability, paired with soft, rounded rectangles for the desk surface to eliminate harsh, uncomfortable edges.

Technical Specifications

Dimensions

Surface Area: 120cm x 60cm
Base Height: Adjustable pneumatics from 70cm to 120cm
Monitor Shelf: 100cm x 20cm, positioned right at eye-level.

Ergonomic Enhancements

Curved wrist-rest edge, anti-glare matte finish top to reduce eye strain, and a dynamic sloped footrest to properly promote leg circulation during studying sessions.

Build Materials

Constructed with a sustainable bamboo core for maximum durability, wrapped in a smooth touch carbon fiber texture, supported by aerospace-grade aluminum "Line" leg beams.

Integrated Connectivity

Concealed magnetic cable management channels, 2x built-in USB-C fast charging ports under the lip, and 1x integrated flush wireless charging pad on the surface.
